About

Meet Ellie

Hi, I'm Ellie! I'm a qualified dog groomer based in Felinfoel, Llanelli, and I absolutely love what I do. Every dog that comes through my door gets my full, undivided attention — it's always just me and your pup, one at a time.

I set up Bark & Bubbles because I wanted to create a calm, relaxed space where dogs feel safe and comfortable. No rush, no stress — just a proper pamper session tailored to your dog's needs. Whether it's a full groom or just a bath and tidy, your furry friend is in safe hands.
