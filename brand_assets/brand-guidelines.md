# Bark & Bubbles by Ellie — Brand Guidelines

## Colours

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary Background | Blush Pink | `#F5D5CC` | Hero backgrounds, section accents |
| Primary Text / Logo | Warm Brown | `#5C3D2E` | Headings, logo text, icons |
| Accent | Soft Rose | `#E8A99A` | Buttons, highlights, hover states |
| Light Background | Cream | `#FFF8F5` | Page background, card backgrounds |
| Dark Text | Espresso | `#3A2518` | Body text, paragraphs |
| White | Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | Card surfaces, contrast areas |
| Subtle Border | Dusty Pink | `#E6C4BA` | Borders, dividers, subtle outlines |

### Colour Notes
- The brand is built around soft pinks and warm browns — pulled directly from the logo.
- NEVER use cool blues, purples, or default Tailwind colours.
- Backgrounds should feel warm and inviting, not clinical white.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Source |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| Headings / Display | **Fraunces** | 600–700 | Google Fonts — warm, rounded serif with personality |
| Body | **DM Sans** | 400–500 | Google Fonts — clean, friendly, highly readable |
| Accent / Labels | **DM Sans** | 500, uppercase, letter-spaced | Same as body but styled differently |

### Typography Notes
- Fraunces gives that warm, approachable, slightly playful feel that matches the hand-drawn logo style.
- DM Sans is clean but not cold — pairs well without feeling corporate.
- Headings should be generous in size (2.5rem+ on desktop).
- Never use Inter, Roboto, Arial, or system fonts.

## Tone of Voice
- **Warm and friendly** — like chatting to a neighbour
- **Reassuring** — "your dog is in safe hands"
- **Personal** — Ellie is the business, use her name naturally
- **Local** — proud of Felinfoel/Llanelli, not trying to be a chain
- **Simple** — no jargon, no corporate speak
- Emoji use: sparingly, only paw prints 🐾 or hearts ❤️ if at all

## Logo
- File: `brand_assets/logo.png` (the pink circle logo with Ellie and two dogs)
- Always display on blush pink or cream background
- Don't place on dark backgrounds without a light container
- Minimum size: 48px height for nav, 120px for hero/footer

## Photography Style
- Real photos of dogs Ellie has groomed (from Facebook)
- Candid, natural — not stock photos
- Dogs on the pink grooming table are the signature look
- Use rounded corners (border-radius: 12–16px) on all images

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous padding — nothing should feel cramped
- Rounded corners everywhere (buttons, cards, images)
- Soft shadows with warm tint: `0 4px 20px rgba(92, 61, 46, 0.08)`
- Mobile-first — most of Ellie's customers will find her on their phone via Facebook

## Don'ts
- ❌ No corporate/tech startup aesthetic
- ❌ No sharp corners or harsh shadows
- ❌ No cool-toned greys or blues
- ❌ No stock photos of dogs
- ❌ No complex animations — keep it simple and warm
- ❌ No dark mode — this brand is light and friendly
